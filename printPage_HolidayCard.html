PRINT ART HOLIDAY PACKAGE Print Art is a prominent printing and graphic design company located in southern New Jersey. Wishing to advertise both their graphic design services and their non-conventional printing capabilities a holiday package was designed to be shipped for the Christmas season. The piece included an series of illustrated characters and perforated props in which the recipient could extract and pose with. The photos were then uploaded to social media outlets to help spread both holiday cheer and the awareness of Print Art.
