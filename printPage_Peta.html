PETA PETA has long campaigned for the rights of animals everywhere. While it's easy to sympathize with a rather photogenic animal such as a rabbit or deer, fish are another matter. Fish are rather hard to sympathize with since they are so different than most other animals that we would encounter normally. As a result we tried to invoke sympathy regarding the fish's situation The following three pieces were designed to draw attention to several important problems facing sea life and the survival and health of our oceans today such as pollution, overfishing and farm fishing.
